A message straight from our hearts 💌

For Our Papa

Papa, aapke liye “thank you” bolna bohot chhota lagta hai, kyunki aapne humare liye jo kiya hai uska scale words se bohot bada hai. Aapne sirf hume raise nahi kiya, aapne hume protect kiya, guide kiya, support kiya aur bina shor kiye humare liye har roz khade rahe.

Kabhi strict banke, kabhi silent support banke, kabhi shield banke aur kabhi motivation banke, aapne har role itne naturally nibhaya ki shayad bachpan me hume samajh hi nahi aaya ki hum kitne lucky hain. Ab jab memories ko dekhte hain, toh har photo me aapka pyaar visible lagta hai.

Hume aapse bohot kuch seekhne ko mila hai — zimmedari, strength, patience, family ko first rakhna, aur bina show-off ke sabke liye present rehna. Agar hum kabhi life me strong ban paaye, grounded rahe, ya kisi ke liye reliable ban paaye… usme aapka hi reflection hoga.

Papa, aap sirf humare father nahi ho. Aap humare hero ho, humari comfort place ho, humari respect ho, aur humari life ke sabse important people me se ek ho. Hum aapse bohot pyaar karte hain 🤎

With all our love,

Virat & Samrat

Happy Father’s Day, Papa 🌼
